networkPoetics

Being a network poet is lots of fun, but it's a kind of fun that takes lots of effort.

network poets (and creative people in general) should never be bored or have to drudge at stupid repetitive work, because when this happens it means they aren't doing what only they can do — solve new problems. This wastefulness hurts everybody. Therefore boredom and drudgery are not just unpleasant but actually evil.
